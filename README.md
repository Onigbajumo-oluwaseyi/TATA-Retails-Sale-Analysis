# My Online Retail Store Business Performance Analysis

**Author:** Onigbajumo Oluwaseyi Olamide 

# Project Overview
I analyzed the performance of an online retail store to provide actionable insights to the CEO and CMO. My analysis focuses on:  
- Revenue drivers  
- Marketing effectiveness  
- Customer demographics  
- Operational efficiency  

The goal of my project was to guide **strategic planning** and support data-driven decisions for the upcoming year.

# Tools I Used
- Microsoft Excel  
- Pivot Tables & Charts  
- KPI Analysis  
- Dashboard Creation

# My Data Description
The dataset I worked with includes:  
- Product categories  
- Customer information (known/unknown)  
- Sales transactions and quantities  
- Returns and return types  
- Order details  
- Geographic information  


# Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) I Calculated
- Total Revenue  
- Total Quantity Sold  
- Total Customers  
- Total Orders  
- Average Order Value (AOV)  
- Return Types  


# Visualizations & Dashboards I Created
- **Geographical Performance** – Revenue and orders by region  
- **Customer Purchase Insights** – Known vs unknown customers  
- **Revenue vs Quantity Trend** – Track revenue growth vs sales volume  
- **Revenue Analysis by Month** – Monthly trends and seasonality  
- **Return vs Sales Analysis** – Measure impact of returns  
- **Top 10 Product Performance** – Highlight best-selling products  


# Key Insights I Found
- Identified top performing product categories  
- Highlighted high value customer segments  
- Measured marketing effectiveness and impact of returns  
- Discovered revenue trends that indicate growth opportunities  
- Recognized geographic regions with high sales potential  

# My Recommendations
- Focus on top-performing product categories for inventory and promotions  
- Target high-value customer segments with personalized marketing campaigns  
- Optimize marketing spend based on channel performance  
- Reduce return rates through improved product information and quality control  
- Explore geographic regions with strong sales potential for expansion  


# Next Steps I Suggest
- Implement continuous KPI tracking to monitor performance in real-time  
- Integrate additional data sources (e.g., website analytics, social media) for deeper insights  
- Automate dashboards and reporting for the management team  
- Conduct quarterly reviews to assess progress and adjust strategies  


# Conclusion
Through this project, I demonstrated my ability to clean, analyze, and visualize retail data using Excel dashboards and KPI metrics. My insights and recommendations provide a **data-driven foundation** for strategic decision-making, operational efficiency, and long-term business growth.
